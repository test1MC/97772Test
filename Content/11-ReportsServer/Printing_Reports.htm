Printing Reports

You print reports directly from your web browser. In any web browser, there are a number of options you can change to control the way your report appears.

Providing detailed information on the print options provided by most popular browsers is beyond the scope of this guide. Here are some options you want to look for. In most browsers, these options are available from a Page Setup window.

  • Paper size
  • Orientation (portrait or landscape)
  • Page scaling (to fit page width or height in current orientation)
  • Headers
  • Footers
  • Page numbers
  • Print date
  • Font
